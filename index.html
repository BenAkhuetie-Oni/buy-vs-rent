Methodology (what this tool is doing)
  • Out-of-pocket includes all monthly costs plus closing costs (buy + sell) and down payment cash outlay.
  • Equity (buy) = down payment (initial equity) + total principal paid + home appreciation + invested monthly savings (if buying costs less than renting).
  • Equity (rent) = invested monthly savings (if renting costs less than buying).
  • Net Worth Impact = Equity − Out-of-pocket (computed each year, including year 0).
